SCRAMBLE FOR AFRICA

4-Player Board Game — Ages of Imperialism

How Many Human Players?

Objective — 15 Rounds

Control resources and earn Prestige. Highest combined score wins!

Resources (per collection)

  • 🪨 Coal = 4 pts  |  💎 Diamonds = 4 pts
  • 💰 Gold = 3 pts  |  ⚙️ Iron = 2 pts  |  🧂 Salt = 1 pt

Prestige ★ (new!)

  • First to claim a territory: +1 ★
  • Build a factory: +2 ★ | Win combat: +1 ★
  • Coastal territory held each turn: +1 ★
  • Control full subregion: +2 ★ per turn

Export Routes (new!)

🚢 If you hold NO coastal territory, your collection is halved each round!

African Resistance (new!)

🛡️ Ethiopia, Ghana, and South Africa resist entry — roll the required number or be repelled.

Berlin Conference — Round 5 (new!)

🏛️ Factories without a pawn present are removed. Effective occupation required!

Event Cards (new!)

📋 A historical event card is drawn each round, affecting all empires.
